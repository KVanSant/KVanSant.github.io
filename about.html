#PassThePen & Collab TV

Pass The Pen began in March of 2018 with the hope of collaborating and working with others in CodePen's worldwide community. From #PassThePen grew Collab TV, a Twitch channel focused on live streaming collaborative projects while they're happening. The first stream, a Collab Mode Extravaganza, was held on September 6th, 2019. These collaborations have been amazing experiences. I'm grateful to everyone who has been a part of them! Thanks y'all!
